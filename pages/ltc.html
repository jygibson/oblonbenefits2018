Long Term Care

Long Term Care (LTC) Insurance helps manage the costs of long term care services.

Important Open Enrollment Notice: Long Term Care is not part of Open Enrollment. If you would like to cancel your coverage during Open Enrollment, please contact Human Resources.


What is Long Term Care?

Long Term Care is offered through LifeSecure. This benefit is only offered to new hires within their first 90 days. Long Term Care provides benefits to help manage the cost of long term care services as needed in case of disability or chronic disease that impairs a personâ€™s ability to perform normal daily activities. Overall services may include home healthcare, assisted living and/or skilled nursing care or living situations. Coverage purchased through the Firm does not require a prequalifying medical evaluation. Under this plan, your spouse may also purchase a policy at the group rate, plus there is the added benefit of further reduced premiums if both spouses participate in the plan.

How Can I Enroll?

New hires (or newly eligible employees) or will have 90 days from their hire date to enroll in Long Term Care. If you would like to enroll, or learn more about how much the personalized plan would cost you, click here to email Human Resources for our broker's contact information.
