Pet Insurance

Health insurance to cover Fido too!

Pet insurance is offered through Nationwide. Your furry family members may have some health needs as well, which is where pet insurance can help out. Pet insurance also has a deductible ($250), and then covers 90% of any veterinary bills. One big difference between pet insurance and regular health insurance (one of many!) is that it's all reimbursement based. You will first pay out of pocket for any veterinary bills, file the claim, and receive reimbursement from Nationwide. You can enroll or drop pet insurance any time of the year, and the premiums vary based on type of pet, age, and location.

To get more information or to sign up, either visit PetsNationwide.com or call 877-738-7874.

Click HERE to download Pet Insurance flyer from Nationwide. The flyer will show you what kind of items are covered, different plan coverages, and where to go to sign up. You will need to contact Nationwide for an accurate quote for your pet.
