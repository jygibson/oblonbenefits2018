Eligibility

Check out the contact page if you have any questions.

Your Eligibility

You are eligible to enroll in benefits if:

  • Full Time Employees: Full time employees are those who work 30 hours or more a week. They are eligible to enroll in all benefits described here. Full time employees can also enroll their eligible dependents in most plans.
  • Part Time Employees(22.5 hours per week): Part time employees who work between 22.5 hours and 30 hours a week are eligible to enroll in the 401k/profit sharing plan once they reach the entry guidelines.
  • Part Time Employees(less than 22.5 hours per week): Part time employees who work less than 22.5 hours a week are not eligible for benefits.
Your Dependents

You can enroll the following dependents onto your plans:

  • Your spouse
  • Your dependent children

An eligible dependent child must be under age 26* and meet one of the following plan requirements:

  • Be your biological child(ren);
  • Be your step-child(ren);
  • Be your legally adopted child(ren);
  • Be your foster child(ren), including any child(ren) placed with you for adoption;
  • Be any child(ren) you are responsible for under court order;
  • Be your grandchild(ren) in your court-ordered custody.
* Coverage for a handicapped child may be continued past the age limits shown above - see Human Resources directly for more information.
