Teladoc

Teladoc is what's commonly known as telemedicine. You can reach a doctor by phone or video for $40 a visit. It also includes mental health visits.

What is Teladoc?

Teladoc allows you talk to a doctor, therapist, or medical expert by phone or video, wherever you are. Teladoc services are available to you through enrollment in Oblon's medical plans, at no extra cost. They allow you to see a doctor (in network) without leaving the comfort of your home. They also offer mental health services as well.

How Does Teladoc Work?

You create an account with Teladoc and then request an appointment. You can choose phone or video, although they may prefer video to see your symptoms. Each Teladoc visit has a co-pay of $40. However, if your condition is too serious, the doctor may recommend you physically go to see a doctor, which will cost whatever that doctor charges as well. Examples of when to call Teladoc are:

  • Flu, sinus infections, stomachaches
  • Dermatology
  • Pink eye
  • Talk to a Therapist or Psychiatrist
  • and more!
