Firm Paid Benefits

The following benefits are provided to you by the Firm.

Group Term Life Insurance

Group Term Life Insurance is provided through MetLife. The Firm pays the premiums for group life insurance coverage equal to two times the employee’s annual earnings, up to $500,000. According to IRS regulations, participants are responsible for paying taxes on any imputed income resulting from life insurance coverage in excess of $50,000. For those who prefer not to incur this tax liability, they have the option to opt out of coverage in excess of $50,000 when they are first hired and also during annual Open Enrollment periods. Those who opt out, however, cannot later increase their firm-paid coverage without going through full medical underwriting. Participants will also receive the same amount of Accidental Death and Dismemberment (AD&D) coverage as they do in Life Insurance, with premiums also paid by the Firm.

Short Term Disability

Short Term Disability is Firm-Provided. Please see the Oblon Policy Manual for the specifics that apply to you.

Long Term Disability

Long Term Disability is provided through Unum. Long-term disability (LTD) coverage provides 60% of basic monthly earnings, up to a maximum of $25,000 per month in the case of an employee’s own serious illness/injury that keeps them from being able to perform in their job. The LTD benefit is activated after a requisite elimination period (continuous absence) equaling 90 days of disability.

Employee Assistance Program

The EAP is provided through Inova. All employees have immediate access to the Employee Assistance Program through INOVA™ EAP and through the www.inova.org/eap website (user name: oblon / password: oblon). INOVA™ EAP offers free and confidential counseling to all who work at Oblon as well as access to a myriad of other helpful services, which in some circumstances may also be available to family members.


Please refer to Oblon Policy Manual for information on benefits such as vacation and sick leave, PTO, FMLA, holidays, and much more valuable information.
