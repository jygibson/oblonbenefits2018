Critical Illness Insurance

Critical Illness insurance helps you face the costs of unexpected serious illness.

What is Critical Illness Insurance?

Critical Illness insurance is offered through AllState. Critical Illness insurance is not a replacement for health insurance, it is designed to help you pay your bills and take care of yourself and family in case the unexpected occurs. When diagnosed with a critical illness, a lot of unexpected costs are involved (and not just for medical needs). Critical Illness coverage offers financial support if you are diagnosed.

How does Critical Illness Insurance Work?

Critical Illness insurance will pay out cash benefits directly to you, of a value that corresponds with your diagnosis and elections. You can then, in turn, use the cash value however you would like. For example, you could use it to:

  • Pay the medical bills from illness
  • Pay regular bills, if you end up out of work
  • Put it in your savings account for another time
  • It's yours to do with as you please!
When electing critical illness coverage, you must choose the value of coverage you would like ($15,000 or $30,000), and must choose between being a tobacco user or a non-tobacco user. You should select the "tobacco" coverage if anyone to be insured has used tobacco products within the last 12 months. Keep reading below for the semi-monthly premiums and full plan flyer.
