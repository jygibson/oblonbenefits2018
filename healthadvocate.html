Health Advocate

Health Advocate is your lifeline for navigating the healthcare and insurance maze.

Health Advocate is great resource provided to you by the Firm. There's no additional fee - the services are provided to all employees, whether you have Firm insurance or not*. The program is designed to help you deal with health insurance and other health stuff, as those can often be difficult to navigate on your own.

What are some examples of what Health Advocate can do for me?


Health Advocate provides a large array of assistance for you and your family. They can:

  • Help to find in-network doctors, dentists, and other providers
  • Help schedule appointments for you and arrange for tests
  • Answer questions about test results
  • Facilitate the transfer of your medical records, x-rays, or lab results
  • Explain your coverage
  • Help you get approval for covered services and identify alternatives for non-covered services
  • Help with medical bills (including finding errors in bills, negotiating fees, help with appeals and more
  • Help with eldercare services like finding in-home care or assisted living
  • Clarify Medicare and Medicaid
  • and more!!

What happens when I call?

During your first call, you will be assigned a "Personal Health Advocate" to help with your case. Personal Health Advocates are registered nurses, who are in turn supported by medical directors and benefits and claims specialists.

You will be asked to complete a form in order for the Personal Health Advocate to assist you. This form allows them to work on your behalf and have access to your medical records. The Personal Health Advocate cannot get much done without completing this form.

What else should I know?

All Firm employees and their family members have free use of this benefit, including tax dependents, parents, and parent-in-laws! You can reach out today either online by clicking HERE or by calling (toll free) 866-695-8622.



* Please note that Health Advocate only has Oblon plan information on file. If a family member that is not covered under Oblon insurance is calling, they will likely need to provide their plan information for the Health Advocate to be able to assist.
