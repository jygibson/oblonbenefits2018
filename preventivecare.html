Preventive Care

Preventive care is covered under our plans at 100% (in-network). These are routine health care services that include annual physicals, screenings and counselings to prevent illness, disease, or other health problems.

Preventive care is covered in-network at 100% under the Affordable Care Act. This feature of our medical plans is designed to keep you healthy and help ward off future issues. Typically these services are included in your annual physical, but a few of them may not be.


Please be aware though that if you go to a doctor for your annual physical and they find an issue, further investigation into what's wrong is not preventive care. At that point, services become diagnostic. Deductibles, co-insurance, and co-pays apply when diagnostic procedures or treatment for an illness/injury begins.


There are limitations on preventive care. Aetna follows the recommendations of national medical societies to determine when and how often individuals need to have these preventive care services taken care of. If you have a preventive care service outside your eligibility (for example, a colonoscopy before age 50), the procedure will not be covered as preventive.
