Supplemental Life and AD&D Insurance

Life and AD&D Insurance to boost your Firm-paid coverage.

Supplemental Life andn AD&D Insurance is offered through Unum. The Firm provides employees with Group Basic Life and AD&D coverage up to 2 times their annual earnings. If you would like to cover yourself at a higher value, you can enroll in Supplemental Life and AD&D. With the Unum Supplemental Life, you can also cover your spouse and children at an equivalent or less value to your coverage.

How Much Coverage Can I Get?

You can enroll for up to 5 times your salary in coverage, not to exceed $1,000,000. You chosen value will need to be $10,000 increments. You have a guarantee issue (how much coverage you are able to get without medical underwriting) of $250,000. For AD&D, you can elect up 5 times your salary to $1,000,000 in $10,000 increments as well, but allamount of AD&D are guarantee issue.

What About for My Family?

You can enroll a spouse for 100% (or less) of the amount you elected for yourself in both life and AD&D. The guarantee issue for a spouse is $25,000. Children have a few different limitations:

  • Live birth to 14 days: $1,000
  • 14 days to 6 months: $1,000
  • 6 months to 19 years(25 years if a full-time student): $10,000 in increments of $2,000.
