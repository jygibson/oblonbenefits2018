Legal Insurance

Legal assistance without the costly attorney fees.

What is Legal Insurance?

Legal insurance is offered through ARAG. Legal insurance helps you deal with everyday situations that occur like dealing with traffic tickets, buying a home, creating a will and even divorce.

How does Legal Insurance work?

ARAG connects you with an experience attorney in your area who will help you review and prepare documents, advise you on legal issues, and even represent you in court. There's also a bunch of online resources for you to put to work as well. Attorney fees can cost as much as $323 an hour, but with ARAG you can get legal assistance for only $18.00 a pay period ($432 a year!).
