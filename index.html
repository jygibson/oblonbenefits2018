Oblon Benefits 2018-2019

Oblon cares about the health and total wellness of it's employees and is dedicated to offering a competitive benefits package for you and your family. As a full-time employee, you are likely eligible for a variety of benefits you will want to learn about, including medical/pharmacy, dental, and vision, all of which are explained in this site. We hope that you and your family find this to be both informative and useful. Please take the time to look through it, and remember that you can always email someone in HR or Human Resources with your questions.

Important Enrollment Dates

New hire enrollments:
New hires are eligible to enroll their first 31 days of employment.

Open enrollment:
Open enrollment this year will take place November 4th - 18th 2018.

Changes:
You can make benefit changes within 31 days of a life-changing event/change of status

Where to enroll: You can make your benefit elections and/or changes at ADP Workforce Now.


Important Open Enrollment 2018 (for 2019) Information


Open enrollment will take place this year from November 4th to November 18th. As in years past, you will be making your annual changes in the ADP Workforce Now portal, which can be accessed from any computer with internet. Click HERE to go to the ADP portal now. If you are unable to remember your username or password, please click on "forgot your user ID/password" and follow the steps to reset.

What's Changing for 2019?

There are no major changes for 2019. You will notice minor premium changes for:

Legal
Premiums decreased by $0.50 to $18.00 per pay period.
Learn more about Legal Insurance here
Dental
6.5% increase, which amounts to only a couple dollars.
Check out the 2019 premiums here
Health
3.2% increase, which amounts to a couple dollars (if even), depending on your salary band and coverage level.
Check out the 2019 premiums for Plan B here and premiums for Plan C here.

What Do I Need to Do?

Open enrollment this year is passive, which means if you want to keep your plans how they are, you do not need to take action EXCEPT FOR:

A Health Savings Account
Your Health Savings Account elections will not roll over into 2019.You must login to ADP and select your contribution for 2019.
For more information on HSAs, check out the HSA page here.
Any Spending Account Enrollments
Standard FSAs, Limited Purpose FSAs, and Dependent Care FSAs do not rollover into 2019. If you would like to enroll in one of these for 2019, you must login to ADP and take action.
For more information on these accounts, check out the FSA page here.
